About Street Dance

Explore the roots, styles, and soul of street dance culture

Street Dance Timeline

1970s

Breaking emerged in New York, blending martial arts and music.

1980s

Street dance hit the mainstream via MTV and movies.

2000s

Competitions and global culture professionalized the scene.

2020+

Street dance is now in the Olympics and continues to evolve.

How to Start?

1

Pick music you vibe with

Start with tracks that spark your interest and make you move.

2

Try beginner tutorials

Watch and imitate to understand basic movements.

3

Practice daily

10 minutes a day builds groove and control over time.

4

Share & join the crew

Post clips, join sessions, and dance with others!
